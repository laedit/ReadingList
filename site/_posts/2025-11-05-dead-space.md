![Couverture](/img/9781781165515.jpeg)On a distant human colony, a dig team uncover something extraordinary: a twisting alien Marker covered in strange symbols. Soon, citizens are suffering - hallucinations, psychosis, paranoia are everywhere - but the worst is yet to come.

As a long-dormant Necromorph threat emeerges and the roots of a Unitologist conspiracy are exposed, P-SEC Sergeant Abraham Neumann finds himself in the middle of a desperate attempt to make it off-planet before all hell breaks loose.
