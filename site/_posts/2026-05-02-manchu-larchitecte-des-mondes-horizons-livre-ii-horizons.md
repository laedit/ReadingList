With Horizons, the journey unfolds. This second volume continues to explore the work of painter Manchu.
Here, the panoramic format comes into its own, confronting the vastness of cosmic landscapes, the monumental scale of spacecraft, and futuristic architectures. Each of the 144 illustrations opens like a window onto the imagination, but also onto science itself, through the depiction of hostile or majestic exoplanets and space missions.
Horizons is an immersive visual experience, an extension of Book I: Odysseys. Together, these two volumes form a landmark editorial work: two complementary art books, a single vision - that of an artist who, for decades, has asserted himself as The Architect of Worlds.
